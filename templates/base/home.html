The chapter has created the first virtual 24/7 Remote Robotics Lab in the country. This feat has never been achieved by any educational institute until now.

The lab enables users from anywhere in the world to control the robots and run developed algorithms remotely, without needing to purchase any hardware. The lab offers a chance to experiment with various features and applications on real robots in a simulated environment with obstacles. The lab can be used for task testing and training in academia and research. The lab will be autonomous and available for use globally in the near future.
