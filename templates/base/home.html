What is the Remote Robotics Lab ?

The Virtual Lab is India's first 24/7 remote robotics lab established by an educational institution. The lab offers an innovative way to provide students with hands-on experience in robotics and automation. Anyone across the globe can control and test the robots from the comfort of their home. Students can learn about robotics without the need for expensive hardware or physical lab space.

Students can conduct experiments, test robotic algorithms, and implement ROS libraries in real time by accessing the Virtual Lab. The Virtual Lab has several practical applications in research and development. Researchers can use the lab to test and refine their robotic algorithms, simulate real-world scenarios, and collaborate with colleagues across the globe.
